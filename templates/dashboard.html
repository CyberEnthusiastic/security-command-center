Push Notifications (ntfy.sh)

Free phone notifications — no account, no password, no email needed

ntfy.sh is a free, open-source push notification service. You pick a secret topic name (like a private channel), subscribe on your phone, and get instant alerts.

Setup (2 minutes, one-time):

Step 1: Install the ntfy app on your phone
    Android: Google Play Store
    iPhone: Apple App Store
Step 2: Open the app, tap "+", and subscribe to your topic name below
Step 3: Enter the SAME topic name below and click "Save"
Step 4: Click "Send Test Notification" to verify

Tip: use something unique like "mohith-security-2026" — not "test" or "alerts"

What you'll receive on your phone:

🚨 CRITICAL: "AI SAST Scanner: 10 CRITICAL findings!" (urgent priority, vibrates)
⚠ Scan complete: "Cloud Misconfig Hunter: scan complete" (normal priority)
❌ Failures: "WAF Bypass Lab: FAILED" (high priority)
✅ System: "Security Suite started" / "Security Suite stopped"

Privacy note: ntfy.sh is open-source (source code). Messages are encrypted in transit (HTTPS). Topic names are not listed publicly — only someone who knows your exact topic name can see your alerts. For maximum privacy, you can self-host ntfy on your own server and change the "ntfy Server" URL above.
